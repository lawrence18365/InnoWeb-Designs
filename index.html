Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

Our design process involves understanding your needs, researching your industry, creating wireframes, designing mockups, and iterating based on your feedback until we achieve the perfect design.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a more complex project could take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and performs optimally long after the initial launch.

What technologies do you use?

We use a variety of modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and WordPress, among others. The specific tech stack is chosen based on your project's unique requirements.
